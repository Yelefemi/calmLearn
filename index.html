Welcome to CalmLearn !!!

Learning does not have to be stressful. CalmLearn compares traditional and guided learning methods on the same topic so researchers can measure time, accuracy, and self-assessment.

Why CalmLearn?

Many students feel pressured by fast lessons and timed tests. CalmLearn creates a safe learning space where understanding matters more than speed.

What To Expect

  • You will complete a short baseline assessment.
  • You will use both learning methods in randomized order.
  • Each method is followed by a short quiz and post-quiz check-in.
  • Your completion data (time, accuracy, self-assessment) is stored for researcher analysis.
  • Individual performance results are not displayed to participants during the study.
