Photosynthesis

Take your time 🌱 Read slowly, understand clearly, and move forward only when you feel ready.

Step 1: What is Photosynthesis?

Photosynthesis is how green plants make their own food using sunlight.

☀️🌿
Plants use sunlight as energy to prepare food.

Step 2: What Do Plants Need?

Plants need sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide to make food.

💧🌬️☀️
These materials come from air, soil, and the sun.

Step 3: Why Is It Important?

Photosynthesis provides food for plants and oxygen for living things.

🌍❤️
Without photosynthesis, humans and animals cannot survive.

Step 4: Sugar Power!

The food plants make is actually sugar. The leaves store it and send it down to the roots, stems and fruits to help the whole plant grow big and strong.

🍬🌿➡️🍎
That sugar is what makes fruits sweet and gives you energy when you eat them!

Step 5: Trees Are Earth’s Helpers

When plants breathe out the extra oxygen, animals and humans use it to breathe in. That’s why forests and grasslands help keep air clean and fresh.

🌳🌲➡️😌
Think of plants as giant green air‑filters that never need cleaning!
